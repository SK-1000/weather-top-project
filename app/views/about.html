Weather Top is a fast, simple and intuitive app for your mobile, with a clean User Interface without those clutters and ads. Get all the information you want at a glance. Getting weather information has never been so easy and amazing. Stay connected to the latest weather conditions with Simple Weather. Compatible with Android Jellybean(4.2.x) and above. App best experienced with Android Lollipop and above (5.0+). Now available in 11 languages: English, Basque, Chinese, Danish, Hindi, French, Norwegian, Dutch, Spanish, Japanese, Russian

Weather Top provides weather reports for the legal, insurance and engineering professions or from private enquirers. A report is produced by a professional Meteorologist who examines all relevant information and gives an opinion on the actual weather. The Legal Weather Report Request form is available here.
